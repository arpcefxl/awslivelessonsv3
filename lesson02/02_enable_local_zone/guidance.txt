Navigate to the EC2 service dashboard
Click on "EC2 Dashboard" on the left-hand nav
On the far right, under Account Attributes, select Zones
If the region selected has no Local Zones, select another region until you see one (or more)
In us-east-1, Local Zones include Atlanta, Boston and Chicago (as examples)
Click on "Manage" next to one of the Local Zones
Change the radio button from "Disabled" to "Enabled"
Click on "Update Zone Group" to opt into the Local Zone
This Local Zone is now available for subnet creation when working with VPCs
