Navigate to the EC2 service dashboard
Click on "EC2 Dashboard" on the left-hand nav
On the far right, under Account Attributes, select Zones
Local Zones are listed first (these will be described later)
Scroll to the bottom to see the standard Availability Zones
Look at the names to see the Zone IDs vs Logical names
Switch regions to see the Zone IDs in each
Some region Zone IDs may match the actual AZ, others will not
