Switch to an AWS account with a public Route 53 domain already provisioned
Navigate to the ACM dashboard and click "Request a certificate"

Certificate type: Request a public certificate
Click Next
Fully qualified domain name: awslivelessons.YOURDOMAIN
Select validation method: DNS validation
Click Request

View the certificate request
Scroll down to Domains and click "Create records in Route 53"
Click "Create records"
View the certificate list, and within a few minutes, the certificate will be issued
